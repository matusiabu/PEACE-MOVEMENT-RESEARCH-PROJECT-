# 3.1 Government approved sign

I started to think about how my system can be displayed. Looking back on the dutch government boards, I tried to see if a concrete base would make sense. Then by joining the plexiglass baseplates with long bolts and nuts, I can create a spiderweb stacking system. I think this is quite fitting because of the metal net that is created and visible through the baseplates. In this arrangement, the plexiglass plates can be seen from every angle and the main peace composition would rest in the front middle, making it only fully visible from the front (where people gather). This adds to the concept of the connective man made world catching attention easily where as real mindfulness requires perspective.
